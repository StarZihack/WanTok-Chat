A Safe & Moderated Platform

WanTok is committed to providing a safe and family-friendly environment. We use a two-layer moderation approach combining advanced artificial intelligence with human oversight:

AI-Powered Moderation: Our automated systems monitor video content in real-time and help us:

  • Prevent nudity and sexually explicit content from appearing on the platform
  • Protect minors with strict zero-tolerance policies
  • Quickly respond to user reports with automated verification
  • Instantly detect and block inappropriate behavior

Human Review Team: Our dedicated moderation team provides an additional layer of safety by:

  • Manually reviewing flagged content and user reports
  • Making final decisions on complex cases that AI flags for review
  • Monitoring platform activity to ensure community standards are upheld
  • Continuously improving our automated systems based on real-world patterns

Users who violate our community guidelines are automatically disconnected and may be permanently banned. We believe that everyone deserves a safe space to connect with others, and we work hard every day to make that a reality.
