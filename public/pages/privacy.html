Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Quick Summary:

WanTok respects your privacy. We collect minimal data (account info, cookies, connection logs) to provide our video chat service. We don't sell your personal information. You control your data, and you can delete your account anytime. Chats are not recorded or stored.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WanTok ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our random video chat platform at wantok.chat (the "Service").

By using WanTok, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Service:

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, full name, username, password (encrypted), date of birth, gender, and country.
  • Profile Information: Any optional information you choose to add to your profile.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase tokens, we collect payment data through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store your credit card details.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We track how you interact with the Service, including pages visited, features used, time spent, and chat connection logs (start/end times, not content).
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to recognize you, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
  • Video/Audio Streams: During video chats, your video and audio are transmitted peer-to-peer. We do not record, store, or monitor your video/audio conversations. Connections are facilitated through WebRTC technology.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Analytics Services: We use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how users interact with our Service.
  • Advertising Partners: We may use advertising networks (e.g., Google AdSense) that collect information about your browsing activity to serve relevant ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To create and manage your account, facilitate video chat connections, and enable core features.
  • Personalization: To match you with appropriate chat partners based on filters (gender, location) if you enable them.
  • Communication: To send you account-related notifications, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Safety and Security: To detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect users from harmful behavior.
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage trends, improve features, fix bugs, and optimize performance.
  • Marketing: To send promotional emails about new features or offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and protect our rights.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, gender, age, and country may be visible to chat partners during conversations.
  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the Service (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors, analytics services). They are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Advertising Partners: We may share anonymized or aggregated data with advertising networks to serve relevant ads. They may also collect data directly through cookies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of WanTok or others.
  • Business Transfers: If WanTok is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

Chat Content: We do not store chat messages, video, or audio. Once a conversation ends, the content is permanently deleted.

6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Update: You can view and update your account information anytime through your profile settings.
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your personal data from our systems.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or updating your preferences.
  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly without them.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@wantok.chat.
  • Do Not Track: We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encrypting passwords and sensitive data
  • Using secure HTTPS connections
  • Regularly updating security protocols
  • Limiting employee access to personal information

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Automated Content Moderation and Safety

To maintain a safe environment for all users, WanTok employs automated content moderation using artificial intelligence technology. Here's how it works:

8.1 Automated Nudity and Inappropriate Content Detection

  • AI Screening: We use third-party AI services (such as Sightengine) to automatically analyze video frames during chat sessions to detect nudity, sexually explicit content, weapons, drugs, and other inappropriate material.
  • When Screening Occurs: Automated checks may occur when you first connect to a chat and when another user reports inappropriate behavior.
  • Screenshot Capture: When moderation checks are performed, a single frame (screenshot) from your video stream may be temporarily captured and sent to our AI service provider for analysis. These screenshots are analyzed immediately and are not permanently stored by WanTok.
  • Automatic Disconnection: If inappropriate content is detected above our safety thresholds, you will be automatically disconnected from the chat and may be temporarily or permanently banned from the Service.

8.2 Minor Protection

  • Zero Tolerance: Any content that may involve minors in inappropriate situations is treated as strictly prohibited and unsafe. Our AI systems are configured with very strict thresholds for such content.
  • Immediate Action: Users displaying or involved in content that may endanger minors will be immediately banned and reported to appropriate authorities as required by law.
  • Age Verification: We require all users to verify they are 18 years or older. Content involving individuals who appear to be minors is flagged for immediate review and action.

8.3 User Reporting System

  • Report Feature: Users can report inappropriate behavior by clicking the "Report" button during or after a chat.
  • AI-Assisted Review: When you report another user for nudity or minor-related concerns, we may automatically capture a screenshot of their video and analyze it using AI to verify the report.
  • Human Moderation Team: All reports are logged and reviewed by our dedicated human moderation team. Reports confirmed by AI may result in immediate automated action, while reports that require additional context are manually reviewed by trained moderators who make final decisions on enforcement actions.
  • Reporter Privacy: Your identity as a reporter is kept confidential and is not shared with the reported user.

8.4 How Screenshots Are Handled

  • Purpose: Screenshots are captured solely for the purpose of content moderation and safety enforcement.
  • Temporary Storage: Screenshots are sent to our AI provider (Sightengine) for real-time analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers or by the AI provider after analysis is complete.
  • No Recording: We do not record full video or audio conversations. Only single frames are captured when moderation checks are triggered.
  • Third-Party Processing: Sightengine processes screenshots in accordance with their privacy policy. They do not use your images for purposes other than providing moderation services.

8.5 Your Consent

By using WanTok, you consent to automated content moderation, including the temporary capture and analysis of video frames for safety purposes. If you do not consent to this moderation, you should not use the Service.
